About Konkan

Konkan is a stunning coastal belt of Western India, stretching from Maharashtra to Goa and Karnataka. With pristine beaches, lush green hills, ancient forts, vibrant villages, and world-famous food, Konkan is a paradise for explorers, families, and foodies alike.
Best Time to Visit: October to March for beaches, July-September for monsoon magic.

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Ganpatipule: Iconic beach temple and tranquil sands.
  • Alibaug: Beaches, forts, and water sports near Mumbai.
  • Ratnagiri: Home of Alphonso mangoes and historic sites.
  • Malvan: Sindhudurg Fort, snorkeling, and seafood.
  • Dapoli: Hill station by the sea, dolphin watching.
  • Murud-Janjira: Legendary sea fort and scenic beaches.
  • Goa: Sun, sand, nightlife, and Indo-Portuguese culture.

Konkan Cuisine

  • Fish Thali: Assorted seafood platter with rice and local flavors.
  • Kombdi Vade: Spicy chicken curry with fried vade bread.
  • Sol Kadhi: Refreshing kokum and coconut drink.
  • Alphonso Mangoes: The king of fruits, best enjoyed in summer.
  • Modak: Sweet dumpling, a signature festive treat.
